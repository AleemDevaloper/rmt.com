Our Story

Rate My Teacher

RMT is about helping students answer a single question "what do I need to know to maximize my chance of success in a given class?" Every teacher and class are different, and knowing what to expect can help students best prepare themselves to succeed.

Rate My Teachers and its predecessors have been around in one form or another since 2001, making it one of the one of the longest running educational websites on the Internet.
